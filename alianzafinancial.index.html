The Process

How it works

Getting started is easier than you think. Everything happens remotely — no office visits required.

1

Book a free 15-minute call

We talk about your business, where things stand with your books, and what you actually need. No pressure, no sales pitch — just an honest conversation to see if we're a good fit.

2

We send you a simple agreement and get access

You sign a short engagement letter, we set up your accounting software, and you share read-only access to your bank statements. Takes about 15 minutes on your end.

3

We handle everything from there

Every month, your books get reconciled and you receive a clear report. You focus on running your business. We handle the numbers. No more dreading your finances.

4

Tax season becomes just another month

Because your books are clean all year, filing is simple. No scrambling. No surprises. No overpaying. Just a straightforward filing with every deduction accounted for.
